It was Into the Wild, the famous and acclaimed movie that planted the seed of travel and especially solo travel in my mind. Engineering gave us a lot of time to watch good movies. A few movies like 'Into the Wild' leave some lasting impression on your mind that you decide to do something you'd normally have not done.

<img src="https://i.pinimg.com/originals/80/6c/ed/806ced6ccecefd3a6c3e2b9696586360.jpg" />

Participating in ACM ICPC (A programming competition) allowed me to travel to various places like Gwalior, Chennai, Bangalore, Mysore, Ooty, Coonor, Coimbatore, etc with my team. We used to travel 2-3 days before and/or after our onsite programming competition. As we used to participate and qualify for ICPC Regionals in South India, I had pretty much explored a large amount of South Indian places.
